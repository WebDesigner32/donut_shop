Data Input Form

This form will allow you to enter a new location for a Top Pot Donuts shop with numeric data. Your input will be appended to the table as a new row. If you click Submit continously or the Enter key after first clicking Submit, you will get new results for the table data. Finally, you can delete what you added using the Erase button located just under the table.
